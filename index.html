Raising Quality Whitetails in the Flint Hills

Sand Creek Whitetail is a whitetail deer breeding operation located in St. George, Kansas. We are dedicated to producing superior genetics and raising healthy, high-quality deer. As active members of the Kansas Elk and Deer Association, we are committed to advancing the captive cervid industry in Kansas.

Quality Genetics

Carefully selected bloodlines focused on antler growth, body size, and overall health.

Kansas Raised

Our deer are born and raised in the Flint Hills of Kansas, adapted to the local climate and conditions.

Industry Involved

Proud members of KEDA, contributing to the growth and integrity of deer breeding in Kansas.
